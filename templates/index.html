Avinash Subramaniam*, Debottam Dutta*, Chaitanya Amballa*

Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering

University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign

* = Equal contribution

Abstract:

In this paper, we present Click2Hear, an interactive spatial audio separation and localization system that utilizes visual cues to improve audio separation and localization accuracy.
